Why magic link?

🔗

A magic link is a secure one-time login link sent to your email, so you can sign in without a password.

📊

Magic links are a passwordless login method that sends a unique, one-time URL to a user's email. Users simply click the link to instantly verify their identity and access their account. They are used to eliminate password fatigue, improve login security, and reduce user drop-off
