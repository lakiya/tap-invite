Design Decisions — What Changed & Why

AddGuestFormComponent

3 fields: Name (required) + Phone (optional) + Email (optional). Inline hint text under each optional field explains its purpose. Validation warning fires if both phone and email are empty.

GuestTableComponent

New Contact column shows icon pair (📱 / ✉️) so host sees at a glance what's available. ✉ Email button only renders when guest.email is present — otherwise shows a muted "no email" label.

Send Email Action

Clicking ✉ Email calls sendEmailInvitation(guestId) on the dashboard. A Supabase Edge Function (or email service) fires and a success toast confirms. The button disables during send to prevent double-sends.
