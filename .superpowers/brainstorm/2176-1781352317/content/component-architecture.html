Approach C
Full Atomic Split
Every visual element its own component
core/services/ ├─ ThemeServicenew └─ Supabase shared/components/ ├─ HeaderComponent ├─ ToastComponent ├─ BadgeComponent ├─ ButtonComponent └─ CardComponent features/host-dashboard/ ├─ HostDashboardComponent ├─ EventFormComponent ├─ AddGuestFormComponent ├─ GuestTableComponent └─ GuestRowComponent features/guest-view/ ├─ GuestViewComponent ├─ InvitationCardComponent ├─ EventDetailsComponent └─ RsvpButtonsComponent

Pros

  • Maximum reusability
  • Design system ready
  • Fully isolated units

Cons

  • Significant overhead now
  • Premature for V1 app
  • Many files for little gain
  • Over-engineering risk

Click to select, then confirm in the terminal. Approach B is recommended — it gives real SRP benefits without over-engineering.
